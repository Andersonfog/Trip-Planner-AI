Trip Planner

An AI-powered travel companion that plans your trip and tells you exactly what to pack.

Live Demo: https://e42f8fc1-157b-4c9b-82d6-040987c19c11-00-1cijyipbbj3ho.riker.replit.dev

What it does

Trip Planner builds a personalized itinerary for your destination and generates a smart packing list tailored to the local weather and time of year. Tell it where and when you're going, and the app factors in seasonal conditions and the forecast to recommend what to bring — from rain gear to layers — so you're never caught unprepared.
